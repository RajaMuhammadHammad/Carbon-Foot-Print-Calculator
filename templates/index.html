Understanding Emission Scopes

Scope 1

Direct Emissions

Direct greenhouse gas emissions from sources owned or controlled by your organization, including fuel combustion in boilers, furnaces, and vehicles. These are emissions you have direct control over.

Scope 2

Indirect Energy Emissions

Indirect emissions from purchased electricity, steam, heat, or cooling. While these emissions occur at the generation facility, they're attributed to your organization based on energy consumption.

Scope 3

Value Chain Emissions

All other indirect emissions in your value chain - from purchased goods and services to end-of-life treatment. Often representing the majority of total emissions, covering 15 GHG Protocol categories.
